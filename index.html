About

I recently graduated from University College Dublin with a BSc in Theoretical Physics. My current interests lie in quantum physics, with a focus on quantum information, quantum thermodynamics and quantum computing. I intend to start a PhD at the Okinawa Institute of Science and Technology. When I'm not studying physics, I enjoy writing, drawing and playing the piano.

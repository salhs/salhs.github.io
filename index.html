Who Am I?

I'm currently a first year PhD student at Okinawa Institute of Science and Technology and an alumni of University College Dublin with a BSc in Theoretical Physics.

My previous projects have examined quantum timekeeping, non-locality in spin chains, maximal work extraction in many body quantum batteries, multi-product formulas for Hamiltonian simulation with tensor networks and a quantum repeater protocol.

When I'm not working on physics, I enjoy all forms of art: visual, written and musical. I'm also a big cat lover

If you'd like to learn more about me professionally, you can find my CVCV or contact me through the links below. I will gladly help prospective students to the UCD physics program, OIST Research Internship or general advice for students wondering whether they want to pursue physics!

What's In Store?

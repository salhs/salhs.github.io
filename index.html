About

I graduated from University College Dublin with a BSc in Theoretical Physics. Currently, I am a PhD student at the Okinawa Institute of Science and Technology, studying in the Networked Quantum Devices unit lead by Assoc. Prof. David Elkouss. My research interests lie in quantum information, particularly nonlocality in networks. When I'm not studying physics, I enjoy writing, drawing and playing the piano.
